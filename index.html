Our Mission

At Bright610, we believe that all students should have equal opportunity and support. We work to achieve this by connecting impassioned tutors who provide near-peer support and academic mentorship with students to drive success through self-empowerment.

Our Vision

Our Vision

We saw the need in our community for tutoring and mentorship for underprivileged students and thought to create Bright610 to provide these needed services. 

Our Vision

Our Vision

Our Vision

Our vision is that one day we as a society will not have the issue of educational inequity. We envision a future in which all students, particularly in our community, have equal access to success.

Our Work

Our Vision

Our Work

We provide free educational services for underprivileged students grades 7-12 in the greater Philadelphia area whose learning was affected by COVID-19 by connecting them with one-one-one virtual tutors.
